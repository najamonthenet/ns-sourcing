Our Process

A streamlined, transparent workflow from concept to delivery.

1

Vendor/Factory Identification

Search and vet manufacturers against your product, capability, lead time, and compliance requirements to build a shortlist of reliable partners.

2

Sourcing

Negotiate terms, manage sample development and approvals, and finalize contracts with selected suppliers.

3

Production

Monitor milestones, conduct in-line QC inspections, and resolve issues during production.

4

Critical Path Management

Monitor key production milestones and manage timelines rigorously. Identify and mitigate risks before they impact delivery. Proactive communication keeps everyone aligned.

5

Delivery

Coordinate logistics, manage customs clearance, and ensure on-time delivery. Final QC inspection before handoff.
